Bintray Debian Package Hosting



  1. Change to the pbuilder output directory.

  2. Upload the Debian package(s) to Bintray.

      • bin-deb-upl.sh -d or --debian for debian packages

      • bin-deb-upl.sh -r or --raspbian for raspbian packages

  1. Ensure the process returns a {Message:Success} message.
